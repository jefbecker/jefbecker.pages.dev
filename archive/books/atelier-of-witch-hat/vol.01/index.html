Atelier of Witch Hat Vol. 01


"Little Coco lives with her mother in a village and has always dreamed of becoming a witch. But they say that only those born with the gift of magic can become sorcerers... She had already given up on her dream when she saw the sorcerer Qifrey cast a spell and discovered a secret!

This is the story of despair and hope of a girl who wants to become a witch."
