Atelier of Witch Hat Vol. 10


"Coco and Tartah are bound by a bracelet cast by Ininia, a mysterious witch from the Brimmed Hats who has allied with Kustas, and are forced to take part in the Silver Night Parade.

As both of them work on developing magic worthy of presenting at the event, the duo ends up encountering Dagda again.

But what they didn't expect was to see a magic circle engraved on the musician's chest — a clear violation of witch regulations.

Was the magic born out of desperation?! The Silver Night Festival, filled with stormy emotions, continues!"
