Almanac of Affectionate Memories Vol. 01


"The Almanac of Affectionate Memories is a book of curiosities about all the things that marked the childhood and youth of those who are now over 30. Toys, publications, bars, customs, music, movies, and television programs. If you feel nostalgic for something you once saw or did in your life, there's a good chance it's in the Almanac."
