What Are Feeds/RSS?

RSS, or Real Simple Syndication, is a quick, free, and accessible way to consume content chronologically without needing to visit the original source that produced it. All you need is an RSS reader, where you can add feed links. The goal is to bring the content to the user so they can consume it in whatever way suits them best—whether it's with their preferred font size or style, light or dark background, etc.
