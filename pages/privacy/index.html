Privacy Policy

Privacy is important, and this website takes it seriously. There is no tracking, ads, or data collection.

Data Collection

  • This website does not collect visitor data.
  • Neocities has a generic visit count system that does not generate tracking data.
  • By using the Guestbook or posting a comment on the website, the visitor acknowledges that they have read and understood the comment rules.
  • When contacting, visitors can choose to use a personal email or a privacy tool to mask the address, such as DuckDuckGo Email Protection, Firefox Relay, or any temporary email service.

It is highly recommended that the visitor does not use a personal email, but if they choose to do so, the address will not be shared with third parties. For cases where the visitor opts to contact with a personal email, the stored data will be: 1. Username; 2. Email address. The use of this data will be considered for private and non-commercial use, in accordance with Art. 4, item I of Law 13.709/18 (General Data Protection Law - LGPD).

Cookies
